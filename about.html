Our Story

The flavor of Jamshedpur, served with love.

Local Favorite

Home-Style Food, Restaurant Vibe

Located in the heart of Bhalubasa, Gupshup Family Restaurant started with a simple vision: to provide the families of Jamshedpur with a dining experience that feels like discovering a warm, home-cooked meal but with the excitement and flair of restaurant dining.

We pride ourselves on our rich North Indian curries and our perfectly spiced Indo-Chinese platters. Our chefs use only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients to recreate the vibrant flavors that our patrons have loved for years.

At Gupshup, we strictly adhere to hygiene standards and offer an affordable menu so that great taste is accessible to everyone—from students celebrating their results to families enjoying their Sunday evening together.

  • 100% Hygienic Preparation
  • Affordable Pricing for All
  • Cozy Family Atmosphere
